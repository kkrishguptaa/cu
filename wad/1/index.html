Idea

Hobbit is a social media platform designed to extinguish larpers in the age of AI. This platform focuses on consumption of authentic content. The most important way for a human being to enlight the flames that burn within their passionate hearts is through consumption of media, culture and engagement with the real world.

Plan of Action

The plan of action for Hobbit involves several key steps:

  • A platform where you log the movies, tv shows, books, music, and other media you consume.
  • A platform where you can see what your friends are consuming and what they think about it.
  • A platform where you can post about your happenings in the real world (e.g., going to a concert, hiking, traveling, etc.) and see what your friends are doing in the real world.
